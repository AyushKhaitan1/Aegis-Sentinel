## Slide 2: The Problem (The Human & Economic Cost)
* **Slide Heading:** The Fatal Silos in Heavy Industry
* **Core Bullet Points:**
  - **The Human Toll:** 6,500+ fatal workplace industrial accidents recorded in India in FY2023 (excluding mining/construction).
  - **The Structural Deficit:** warning data exist, but reside in siloed systems (SCADA, CCTV, Permits).
  - **The Coordination Gap:** Over 60% of large manufacturing facilities rely on **manual handoffs** for safety verification (FICCI 2024).
  - **The Tragic Consequence:** Fatal accidents (e.g., January 2025 steel plant coke oven battery gas explosion) occur not from lack of safety sensors, but from the **absence of a unified intelligence layer to act in time.**
* **Visuals:** High-contrast layout. Left side: Red warning statistics. Right side: Diagram showing siloed columns (SCADA, Permits, CCTV) with broken red lines representing failed manual links.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (Aegis Sentinel)
* **Slide Heading:** Fusing Silos into Predictive Safety
* **Core Bullet Points:**
  - **Unified Data Fusion:** Automatically ingests Modbus SCADA telemetry, digital work permits, and visual CCTV feeds.
  - **Predictive Correlative Engine:** Analyzes interactions between separately "normal" conditions to predict compound risk.
  - **Agentic AI Collaboration:** Employs specialized safety agents to coordinate, audit regulations, and execute playbooks.
  - **Immediate Containment:** Compresses critical reaction times from minutes to milliseconds, moving from reactive investigation to **predictive safety**.
* **Visuals:** Center graphic showing SCADA, CCTV, and Permit logs pouring into a central glowing brain ("Aegis Core"), which outputs real-time alerts.

## Slide 4: Fused Telemetry & Digital Twin
* **Slide Heading:** Live Spatial Plant twin

## Slide 6: The Multi-Agent Reasoning Console
* **Slide Heading:** Collaborative AI in the Safety War Room
* **Core Bullet Points:**
  - **Telemetry Agent:** Audits chemical and temperature SCADA registers (Modbus holding registers 40012/40018).
  - **Permit Agent:** Cross-references permit registries and flags hazardous overlaps.
  - **Compliance Agent:** Compiles safety documentation and verifies statutory clauses (OISD, Factories Act).
  - **Emergency Director:** Automates containment playbooks (sirens, isolations, and alerts) when limits are breached.
* **Visuals:** Technical diagram showing the four agents connected to a central blackboard. Include a snippet of the Agent dialogue log showing active debates during a gas leak.

* **Slide Heading:** Semantic Regulatory Intelligence
* **Core Bullet Points:**
  - **Regulatory RAG Database:** Local vector-search index containing:

* **Visuals:** Screenshot of the **Regulatory RAG tab** displaying search results and a snippet of the printable compliance report.

## Slide 8: Automated Emergency Response
* **Slide Heading:** Playbook Execution & Incident Reporting
* **Core Bullet Points:**
  - **Evacuation Sirens:** Triggers local and central alarm loops automatically.
  - **Emergency Alert Dispatches:** Broadcasts SMS warnings to standby rescue crews.
  - **SCADA Valve Isolations:** Shuts down fuel feeds and welding grids to prevent ignition.
  - **Dynamic Evac Paths:** Draws wind-safe exit vectors on the digital twin map.
  - **Statutory Incident Reports:** Pre-fills a Form XI-A report for submittal to PESO/DGMS.
* **Visuals:** Flowchart showing the emergency playbook steps (`Siren -> SMS -> Valve Isolation -> Evac Route -> Form XI-A`).
