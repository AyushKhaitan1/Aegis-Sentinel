# Aegis Sentinel: Demo Video Voiceover Script

This script outlines a **3-to-4 minute spoken narration** for your prototype demo video. Follow the time cues, visual instructions, and spoken text to record your screencast.

---

## Part 1: Introduction & Problem Context (0:00 - 0:45)

* **Visual on screen:** Open the dashboard in its default state, showing the **Digital Twin Map** tab. Slowly point your cursor to the header and the "Composite Risk Index" bar.
* **Spoken Script:**
  > "Hello everyone. Today we are presenting Aegis Sentinel, our B2B safety intelligence platform addressing Problem Statement 1: AI-Powered Industrial Safety Intelligence for Zero-Harm Operations.
  > 
  > In heavy manufacturing, steel plants, and refineries, safety incidents often occur not because sensors fail, but because safety data exists in disconnected silos. In major accidents like the Visakhapatnam Coke Oven Battery incident, gas warning readings existed on local SCADA channels, but manual handoffs delayed critical containment decisions.
  > 
  > Aegis Sentinel resolves this gap. It acts as a unified predictive safety brain, fusing IoT SCADA telemetry, digital work permits, and CCTV camera analytics into a single predictive layer. It runs a Multi-Agent Risk Engine to identify compound threats before they lead to fatalities."

---

## Part 2: Digital Twin Map Walkthrough (0:45 - 1:25)

* **Action on screen:** Point your cursor to the **Digital Twin Map**. Click on one of the worker nodes (e.g., Rajesh Kumar or Sunil Dutt). Show the worker telemetry card sliding up at the bottom. Point out their name, role, heart rate, and PPE compliance status. Close the card.
* **Spoken Script:**
  > "First, let's explore our Live Spatial Digital Twin tab. Aegis Sentinel maps facility blueprint coordinates into an interactive SVG plant layout. We track worker coordinates, live biometric indicators like heart rate, and safety gear compliance.
  > 
  > Here, we see active permit zones mapped directly onto the plant twin. If we click on a worker like Fitter Sunil Dutt, we can inspect his live vitals and PPE status. 
  > 
  > In the corner, a HUD engineering compass and coordinate ticks align our spatial mapping with physical plant layouts."

---

## Part 3: CCTV YOLOv8 Analytics & SCADA IoT Registry (1:25 - 2:15)

* **Action on screen:** Click on the **CCTV CV Feeds** tab. Point your cursor to the bounding boxes. Then click on the **IoT Registry** tab. Point your cursor to the sparkline graphs. Move the temperature or gas sliders on the right panel slightly and point out the sparklines adapting in real-time.
* **Spoken Script:**
  > "If we switch to the CCTV CV Feeds tab, we see our simulated computer vision auditing in action. Our YOLOv8 model processes RTSP video streams to check for hard hats and fall-arrest safety harnesses. Notice the green bounding box around Rajesh, and how the system flags violations.
  > 
  > Under the IoT Registry tab, we display our linked SCADA transducers. The Registry details Modbus/TCP registers and MQTT topics for each sensor. As telemetry is adjusted in the simulator on the right, these real-time SVG sparklines dynamically rise and fall. This is where judges can see the physical integration of sensors and camera streams."

---

## Part 4: Compound Risk & Playbook Simulation (2:15 - 3:15)

* **Action on screen:** On the right simulator panel, click the **🚨 Confined Space Gas Trap** preset button. The dashboard will instantly turn red, trigger a 100% Critical alert, and auto-focus on the **Emergency Playbooks** tab. Point out the checklists running. Click the **Inspect Form XI-A Draft** button.
* **Spoken Script:**
  > "Now, let's simulate a compound safety threat. When we click the 'Confined Space Gas Trap' preset, our risk engine detects a ventilation failure coupled with active H2S gas accumulation while a technician is inside Storage Tank B.
  > 
  > The Composite Risk Index immediately breaches safety margins, hitting 100% Critical. The dashboard sounds alarms and triggers Emergency Playbook Number 09.
  > 
  > Notice the automated execution checklist: sirens sound, emergency SMS alerts are dispatched to rescue crews, SCADA valve isolations shut down fuel lines, and wind-safe evacuation vectors are drawn on the map. The system also compiles a complete PESO Form XI-A Statutory Incident Report, ready for submittal to disaster management authorities."

---

## Part 5: Regulatory RAG Auditor & Conclusion (3:15 - 4:00)

* **Action on screen:** Click on the **RAG Copilot** tab. Click the quick question button: **"What is the maximum allowable LEL for hot work under OISD 105?"** Show the RAG response compiling, highlighting standard citations, and calculating search confidence. Then click **"Run Live Safety Audit"** to show the printable text report.
* **Spoken Script:**
  > "Next, let's look at the Regulatory RAG Auditor. Supervisors can query our safety acts database in natural language. Clicking our preset question on OISD Hot Work guidelines triggers a semantic search. The auditor fetches the exact clause with a 98% confidence score.
  > 
  > With one click on 'Run Live Safety Audit', the platform compiles a printable, downloadable safety audit document detailing current SCADA values, permit registries, and active infractions.
  > 
  > Aegis Sentinel is a complete, scalable safety layer that is easy to integrate with existing industrial setups. It protects workers' lives, avoids expensive operational shutdowns, and automates regulatory auditing. Thank you for your time."

---

## Tips for Recording your Screen & Voice:
1. **Resolution:** Record in full screen 1080p, ensuring text is readable.
2. **Audio:** Use a quiet room and a clear microphone. Keep your tone professional, structured, and confident.
3. **Pacing:** Practice clicking on the tabs and sliders ahead of time so your screen actions line up perfectly with your voice!
